Who is Abdurahmaan?

Abdurahmaan is a highly-disciplined and driven young man that resides in Cape Town, Surrey Estate. A brother to many and a teacher to some, my journey embodies resilience and a commitment to self-improvement. Graduating from Leadership College Primrose Park in 2019, I pursued excellence by rewriting my matric exams in 2020, achieving a bachelor's pass. My determination led me to explore various fields before securing my first job as an Educator Assistant at Habibia Primary School in late 2021. This experience not only enhanced my professional skills but also nurtured personal growth. Despite the contract ending after 11 months, I remained undeterred, transitioning to a role as a cross-trained Sales Executive at YDE and Truworths, where I honed my sales acumen and reignited my passion for technology. Departing in mid-2023, I embarked on a new chapter by discovering Life Choices, a opportunity poised to further enrich my journey of learning and development in the technolgical field.
